Our Purpose

Empowering Turkish students to navigate global education opportunities with confidence

Our Mission

To simplify international education planning by providing Turkish students with reliable, comprehensive guidance on universities, scholarships, and application processes worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted bridge between Turkish students and their global academic aspirations, removing barriers to international education.

Why AllAbroad?

If you are wishing to pursue your higher education, you probably realised Turkish students are expected to go through various admissions processes. If you've come this far, you probably realized that learning the requirements takes unnecessary time, for every single university. AllAbroad provides Turkish students precisely what they need, tailored guides for institutions all around the world.

As someone who personally is navigating through the challenges of studying abroad, I created AllAbroad to provide Turkish students with the guidance I wish I had. Our goal is to make international education accessible to all.

Yaser İnan

Founder
